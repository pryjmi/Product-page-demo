About

What's that?

NFrame = Custom digital frame for NFT art and collectable

Seamlessly curate NFT art and collectibles from device to display with NFrame® technology. Your standard frame reimagined

A digital frame that gives your room, office, or home creative expression. Place it anywhere in your house and watch as your space transforms into a living gallery



Dynamic Display: Gorgeous Full-HD display with AMOLED technology, 16.7 million colors, high contrast, and 180° viewing angle

Timeless Design: Hand-crafted titan frame, sapphire glass = perfect for any wall, plus additional options for custom colors

Smartphone Integration: Instantly display NFTs from anywhere in the room, directly from your iPhone or Android

Built in two sizes (24-inch / 40-inch) and one premium with immense care and attention to craftsmanship. Every unit contains a gorgeous full-HD display and graphics processing
